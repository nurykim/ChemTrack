About this app

This is the ChemTrack usage logging page. Use it to record when you take a reagent from the lab inventory. Your entry is saved directly to the shared lab Google Sheet.

Photo scan

Take or upload a photo of the bottle label. AI reads the name or CAS number and finds the chemical automatically. Requires an API key configured by your lab manager.

QR scan

Point your camera at the ChemTrack QR code on the bottle. The chemical is identified instantly — no API key needed.

Manual search

Type the chemical name or CAS number to search the inventory directly.

Something not working?

Contact your lab manager. This page is pre-configured — you don't need to enter any URLs or keys yourself.
