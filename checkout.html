Help

Use this page to record when you take a reagent from the lab. Your entry saves directly to the shared lab Google Sheet.

Photo scan

Take or upload a photo of the bottle label. AI reads the name or CAS number and identifies the chemical. Requires an API key — if unavailable, use QR or Search instead.

QR scan

Point the camera at the ChemTrack QR sticker on the bottle. Identified instantly — no API key needed. Requires HTTPS (won't work from a local file).

Manual search

Type the chemical name or CAS number to find it from the inventory.

Setup

Tap the gear icon in the top-right to open Setup. Enter the Apps Script URL (required) and optionally the Sheet URL and Anthropic API key. These are saved to this browser only — ask your lab manager for the values.

Something not working?

Open Setup and confirm the Apps Script URL is filled in correctly. If the inventory won't load, contact your lab manager.
